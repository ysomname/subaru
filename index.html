  • 1992 - 2000

    Introduction

    Introduced in 1992 as a replacement for the Leone

    The Impreza features a horizontally opposed, 4 cylinder "Boxer"
    Engine, available in a range of sizes from 1.5 litres to 2.5 litres.

    World Rally Championship

    Unequal exhaust mainfold headers give the Boxer engine a unique sound and character

    The impreza turbo was hugely popular and quickly became the dominant force in Word Rallying, winning 3 consecutive World Rally Championship in 1995 - 1997.

    The Driver

    Most famously driven by the late colin McRae

  • 2000 - 2007

    Introduction

    The all new model 2 Impreza was slighlty larger & heavier than the outgoing model & featured some rather questionable styling in the form of its bug eye headlamps

    2nd Generation Impreza

    There was two door coupe version but it was discountinued

    Various limited edition models were produced for the car enthustic

    The Driver

    British driver Richard Burns won the Rally Championship in his Impreza WRC in 2001 & 2003

  • 2007 - 2011

    Introduction

    The 3rd generation impreza was lauched in naturally aspirated & turbocharged forms as a 5 door hatchback, with a 4 door saloon offered a year later

    First ever Subar Boxer diesel engine introduced

    Pillarless doors, a design trait from the two pervious generations replaced by standard doors to reduce wind noise

    STI OFFERS

    WRX STI verisons features a wider body and track than the standard WRX

  • 2011 - 2014

    Introduction

    This was the hype one smaller, lighter 4th generation actually more spaceious until 2014

    3rd Generation Impreza WRX model countinued until 2014

    For 2015 the Impreza was dropped from WRX models and the high performance verison is now know as the Subaru WRX

    Electric assisted power steering introduced

    268 bhp

    While the subaru has not powered up for a while this was for the handling performance and stability.
